metal products

Due to the high dynamics of our equipment is characterized by low time spent on processing any part. Exceptional equipment performance and minimal costs have a positive effect on the cost-effectiveness of order processing.

Laser cutting

Well-selected reliable components of high quality and modern technologies make it possible to cut sheet metal as efficiently as possible in terms of all three important parameters - speed, quality, cost.

Bending of metal

Qualitative bending of sheet metal directly depends on the accuracy of adjustment of the equipment and its assembly. It is such important characteristics at a high level provided sheet metal in our production.

Laser welding

The device allows to carry out process of laser welding with extraordinary accuracy and quality of a weld. Welding is performed in nitrogen or argon gas medium. Automated wire feed speed up to 17 m / min, which makes the process extremely productive.
