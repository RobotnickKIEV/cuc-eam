About cluster

Central Ukrainian cluster

The mission and tasks of the cluster are determined by the concept of cluster development of IAM, proposed by the Association of "Industrial Automation Enterprises of Ukraine" at the national level. And in line with international best practices.

Our main goal

The main mission of the IAM Central Committee is to unite the efforts of small, medium, and large machine-building enterprises to implement the principles of Industry 4.0 in the production of equipment for green energy ethics and blue energy.

Mission, principles of functioning and directions of cluster development

The IAC Central Committee is based on the cooperation of a wide range of players from 3 economic sectors (Engineering, Automation, Mechanical Engineering) and from various categories of market participants: small and medium business, large enterprises, universities, research institutions and laboratories, incubators, technology transfer centers, etc.

The IAM Central Committee supports and develops R&D activities, which in turn depend on the general macroeconomic and fiscal instruments of the Kyiv region and Ukraine. To maximize economic growth, these tools need to be tailored and foster innovation and development. The primary task of the IAM Central Committee is to systematize the current situation and provide proposals for the development of these instruments.

The structure of the CUC IAM

- Developers, manufacturers and engineering companies in the IT and industrial automation sectors - as the main drivers of innovation "Industry 4.0".
- Industrial enterprises in the field of mechanical engineering.
- Universities and other educational institutions.
- Local Chamber of Commerce.
- Regional authorities and local governments.
- Development funds.

Management structure of the IAC Central Committee

- The General Meeting is the highest governing body of the IAM Central Committee. The meeting approves the development strategy, annual plan and budget, changes in the Charter, etc.
- The General Meeting elects the Board of the IAC Central Executive Committee, whose role in making strategic decisions and changes, control of operational activities
- The IAC Central Committee appoints the executive directorate, which carries out operational management of the cluster.
- The Executive Directorate proposes to the Board a detailed cluster work plan, determines the budget and implements the action plan.
