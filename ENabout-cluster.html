Mission, principles of functioning and directions of cluster development

The IAC Central Committee is based on the cooperation of a wide range of players from 3 economic sectors (Engineering, Automation, Mechanical Engineering) and from various categories of market participants: small and medium business, large enterprises, universities, research institutions and laboratories, incubators, technology transfer centers, etc.

The primary task of the IAM Central Committee is to systematize the current situation and provide proposals for the development of these instruments.

Management structure of the IAC Central Committee

- The General Meeting is the highest governing body of the IAM Central Committee. The meeting approves the development strategy, annual plan and budget, changes in the Charter, etc.
- General Meeting elects IAM Central Committee Board, whose role is to make strategic decisions and changes, control operational activities
- The Board of the IAM Central Executive Committee appoints the executive directorate, which carries out operational management of the cluster.
- The Executive Directorate proposes to the Board a detailed plan of work of the cluster, determines the budget and implements the action plan.
- The full-fledged structure and launch of the IAM Central Executive Committee has been underway since October 2021.
- The IAM Central Committee has its own coordinator, who establishes communication and coordination of actions.

Cluster membership

IAM is free to join / leave new members.

A member of the cluster can be any business entity, legal entity operating within the categories specified in paragraph 2.1, creates and operates in accordance with the laws of Ukraine and agrees to perform the duties assigned to it Statute of the IAM Central Committee (currently this document). Membership in the IAM Central Committee is based on non-discriminatory principles.

The IAC Central Committee establishes 2 categories of membership: valid and partnership.

The status of a "full member of the cluster" is intended for all commercial organizations in the region and provides for membership fees, the amount of which is determined in accordance with the decision of the General Meeting.

The status of "partner" implies free participation in the work of the cluster on mutually beneficial, partnership relations (for example, mutual promotion, provision of services, etc.). Partner status can only be granted to several types of organizations:

- Government agencies and institutions
- Chamber of Commerce and Industry
- National Business Associations
- Institutions of higher education

Cluster membership is valid:

a) for full members - after the relevant decision of the Cluster Executive Board;
b) for members in the status of "partners" - after the relevant decision of the Board of the cluster.

Admission to the CAM IAM is carried out at the monthly Meeting of Participants free of charge. The application for joining the cluster is submitted to the General Manager of the IAM Central Committee Serhiy Shvedchikov, shvedgrey@gmail.com.

Rules of interaction

Cluster members are required to:

• adhere to these provisions of the cluster (hereinafter - the Charter of the IAM cluster) and the decisions of the General Meeting;
• take part in the implementation of cluster development tasks;
• make timely membership and target contributions;
• be guided in their activities by the principles of ethics;
• take care of the good name of the IAM Central Committee.

Follow COOPETITION

• Upon joining the IAC Central Committee, each participant determines the areas of their competence and specialization in which the development of cooperation in the IAM Central Committee is planned. These zones are fixed in the application for joining the IAM Central Committee.
• Each participant of the IAM CEC selects a partner or partners with whom a joint product is created or a joint project is planned (in engineering, R&D, training project, etc.).
• In the case of direct competition in the market, participants in commercial projects, each member of the IAC Central Committee determines the area of ​​interaction and cooperation in those areas where all members of the cluster cooperate. These include the tasks of joint regional development such as education and training, joint exports, development of quality standards and technical standards, development of joint innovation structures, growth of the image of engineering staff and more. Clusters have the right to exclude cooperation with those members of the IAM Central Committee with whom there is direct competition in the areas of such competition.
• The use of information obtained in the framework of cluster activities for personal gain and which harms another member of the cluster may be considered a breach of ethics and is considered at meetings of the Cluster Board.
• Other rules of interaction and avoidance of direct competitive situations within the cluster are determined by the Board and the General Meeting of the IAC Central Committee.

Benefits of participating in the IAM Central Committee

Members enjoy the following benefits of participating in the IAM CEC:

• Use of joint assets of the cluster (website, joint funds, joint promotion, joint stands at exhibitions, etc.).
• Obtaining important information about market opportunities within the region or beyond.
• Growth of image and reputation, mutual PR.
• Obtaining a synergistic effect of cooperation in the development of joint products or projects.
• Better access to innovation and export resources and opportunities, including finding partners and clients abroad.
• Obtaining preferential terms for training and development programs organized by the IAC Central Committee Partners.
• Priority inclusion in grant and other structural programs of export, innovation, regional, industrial and digital development.
