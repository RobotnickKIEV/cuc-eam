Meeting of the management of NVK "Cleaver" with the director of the State Institution "Scientific Hydrophysical Center of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine"

December 17, 2021
Products, services

17.12.2021р. A meeting of the Society's management with the Director of the Scientific Center for Hydrophysics of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine, Corresponding Member of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine O. Shchyptsov took place. As part of the initiatives of the Naval Subcluster, the parties discussed important areas of scientific and technical cooperation in the interests of the Armed Forces of Ukraine and joint participation in the construction of research vessels in 2022-2030 by Ukrainian shipbuilding enterprises.
